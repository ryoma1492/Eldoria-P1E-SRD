
## Draconic Principle

Scales represent immortality through resilience.

This aspect embodies:
- endurance
- defense
- inevitability
- ancient survival

Bearers of Scales often become stubborn, protective, and emotionally difficult to move.

---

# Stage I — Consecrated Hide

## Manifestation

Small patches of scales emerge beneath the skin.

## Abilities

- +2 Natural Armor
- Resist 5 acid damage
- double Consitution bonus or +2 (whichever si higher) when making death throws

---

# Stage II — Sanctuary Scales

## Assimilation

The scales spread visibly across the body.

## Abilities

- +4 Natural Armor
- DR 3/Magic
- Resist 10 acid damage

### Warding Molt
Immediate action:
- allies within 10 ft gain temporary DR 5/acid

---

# Stage III — Ancient Aegis

## Realization

The bearer becomes nearly draconic in durability.

## Abilities

- +6 Natural Armor
- Immunity to fear and paralysis

### Draconic Intercession
Once/day:
- intercept an attack or spell targeting an adjacent ally

---

## Physical Changes

- Thickened skin
- Metallic or stone-like scale growth
- Increased body weight
- Reduced body temperature
