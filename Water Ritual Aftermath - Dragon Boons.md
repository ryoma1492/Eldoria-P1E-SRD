
The draconic corruption carried by the [[Heroes]] was never intended to create six dragons.

It was intended to recreate one.

During the [[Temple Water Ritual]], the corruption threatening to transform [[Captain Killian]] into a vassal of [[Malazar]] was interrupted, divided, and redistributed among the party. Rather than allowing a singular draconic possession to complete, the ritual fractured the essence into six conceptual aspects of draconic existence.

Each aspect represents not merely a physical mutation, but a metaphysical principle tied to the nature of dragons themselves.

As the bearers continue to use and rely upon these powers, the aspects deepen their attunement, physically and spiritually reshaping their hosts.

The powers are not gifts.

They are organs of a larger being attempting to reassemble itself through mortal vessels.

| Aspect | Principle |
|---|---|
| Wings | Freedom |

---

## Structure of the Aspects

Each aspect progresses through three stages of attunement:

### Stage I — Manifestation
The mutation first appears.
Minor physical changes and limited abilities emerge.

### Stage II — Assimilation
The draconic instinct strengthens.
The bearer gains stronger abilities while their personality and body begin visibly changing.

### Stage III — Realization
The aspect fully awakens.
The bearer becomes a living embodiment of that draconic principle.

---

## Known Risks

Every use of these powers strengthens the lingering influence of [[Malazar]].

The [[Deck of Draconic Will]] exists as the primary method through which the corruption attempts to exert influence over its hosts.

Repeated reliance upon the aspects may:
- alter personality
- harm you physically
- damage your armor/weapons
- invoke compulsions
- accelerate mutation
- weaken resistance to [[Malazar]]'s posession
