## Pre-Text:
The [[Heroes]] return from [[Oargull]] to a [[Lowhaven]] that is no longer merely recovering—it is *growing*. Word of the town’s resistance and the restoration of sunlight has spread. Migration has begun.

But something has taken notice.

The influence behind the [[black crystals]] has not receded. It has adapted.

## Current state
- [[Lowhaven]] is expanding rapidly
  - Refugees arriving daily
  - Governance forming under [[Jellybean]]
- [[Captain Killian]] remains unstable after prior events
- [[Zarthus]] is nearby, observing
- The [[Fool’s Curtain]] continues its cycle
- Unknown forces are acting *within* the city

## Leg One – Celebration and Return

The session opens in [[Oargull]].

- [[Mallard Fleetfin]] unveils a new brew:
  - **Lightbringer’s End**
  - Dark, bitter, with a sweet finish
- [[Jellybean]] trades with [[Nibwick]]:
  - Receives magical belt
  - Nibwick receives fish (extremely pleased)

The voyage back to [[Lowhaven]] is calm.

Too calm.

---

## Leg Two – The Alley Ritual

Upon arrival:

- [[Zarthus]] approaches [[Atticus]]
  - Admits he has been watching the town
  - Still irritated over prior theft
  - Leaves without resolution

Moments later—a scream.

### The Scene

A ritual circle in an alley:

- Chalk circle glowing faintly
- [[Captain Killian]]:
  - Eyes closed
  - Walking the perimeter with unnatural precision
- [[Rowan]]:
  - Floating at center
  - Chest carved with symbol:
    - **A throne encircled by scaled wreath**

### Party Interaction

Attempts to interrupt fail:

- Physical disruption blocked
- Magic redirected or avoided
- [[Killian]] moves with unnatural speed

Entering the circle is extremely dangerous:
- [[Caleb]] nearly dies instantly

### The Breaking Point

As something begins to emerge from Rowan’s chest—

**[[Finn]] kills Rowan.**

The ritual collapses.

For a brief moment, visible to all:

> A jagged black crystal forming inside the body.

## Leg Three – Killian’s Dream

[[Captain Killian]] regains himself slowly.

He describes:

- A recurring dream:
  - Flying north beyond the [[Fool’s Curtain]]
  - Drawn to an island
- Beneath it:
  - A second vision
  - A **sunken coral temple**
- The second vision feels:
  - Suppressed
  - Hidden
  - *Feared*

**Insight:**  
Something is not just calling—it is **curating what is seen**.

## Leg Six – The King Arrives

Inside the mayor’s mansion:

- A candle is already lit

At the table:

- [[King Balfour IV]]
- Three towering generals

He already knows about:
- [[Captain Killian]]

### The Offer

- Safe passage through [[Fool’s Curtain]]
- Ceasefire with the Crown

In exchange:
- Deliver a shipment

### Revelation

- The arrangement dates back to [[King Balfour I]]
- It is tied to an **oath protecting Emberfall**

### Outcome

- [[Jellybean]] refuses
- The king:
  - Shows no anger
  - Leaves calmly

**Tone:**  
This was not a threat.  
It was a test—or a confirmation.

## Leg Eleven – The Dragon

An ancient black dragon.

Calm. Expectant.

### Key Dialogue Points

- Recognizes [[Caleb Brightwater]]
- Mentions:
  - A **dragon fang artifact**
  - A binding oath with Emberfall’s kings

### Critical Truth

> The oath protects those under the fang.

> It does **not** protect those within Emberfall.

The dragon departs peacefully.

- Enormous wealth
- New enemies
- A broken understanding of safety

And something ancient has begun to move.
