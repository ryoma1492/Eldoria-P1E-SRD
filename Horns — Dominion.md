## Draconic Principle

Horns embody rulership through fear and authority.

This aspect represents:
- dominion
- hierarchy
- conquest
- draconic sovereignty

Bearers of Horns increasingly perceive themselves as rightful rulers over weaker beings.

Among all aspects, Horns appears most closely tied to the direct will of [[Malazar]].

---

# Stage I — Crowned

## Manifestation

Small horn protrusions emerge from the skull.

## Abilities

- Gore attack (1d6)
- +2 Intimidate
- +2 CMD vs Bull Rush and Trip

Enemies damaged by the gore attack suffer penalties against fear.

---

# Stage II — Heir of Scale

## Assimilation

The horns become impossible to fully conceal.

## Abilities

- Improved Bull Rush
- Gore attack becomes magical
- Charge attacks deal +1d6 damage

### Dominating Rush
Successful charge:
- free Bull Rush
- no attacks of opportunity

Gain minor Frightful Presence.

---

# Stage III — Dragon Crown Ascendant

## Realization

The bearer radiates instinctive draconic authority.

## Abilities

- Immunity to fear
- Frightful Presence aura
- Gore critical multiplier increases to x3

### Cataclysm Charge
Charge through multiple enemies:
- attack each target in a line
- failed saves are knocked prone

### Sovereign Presence
Weak-willed creatures instinctively hesitate, submit, or avert their gaze.

---

## Physical Changes

- Fully developed draconic horns
- Altered facial structure
- Deepened voice resonance
- Eyes occasionally glow during anger

---

## Known Notes

This aspect appears uniquely dangerous.

There is evidence suggesting [[Malazar]] prefers or favors the bearer of Horns above all other aspects.
