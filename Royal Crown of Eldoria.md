
*Faction / Ruling Authority*

## Overview
The Royal Crown of Eldoria is the governing power of the unified kingdom of [[Emberfall]]. Formed after the Great Dragon War, it presents itself as the guarantor of peace, stability, and survival beneath the [[Fool's Curtain]].

Its authority extends over all former kingdoms, including [[Eldoria]], [[Atheria]], and [[Valoria]].

## Structure
- **Monarch:** [[King Balfour IV]]
- **Military:** Royal Navy, Crown Guard, specialized arcane divisions
- **Clergy & Orders:** Includes paladins such as [[Caldus]]
- **Bureaucracy:** Regional governors, inspectors, and Crown caretakers

## Public Doctrine
The Crown teaches that:
- Unity is the only defense against existential threats
- Dangerous knowledge and technology must be controlled
- Sacrifice is sometimes necessary for the greater good

The Crown strictly regulates arcane devices capable of affecting the [[Fool's Curtain]].

## Known Policies
- Heavy taxation to fund defense and research
- Strict oversight of arcane research and inventors
- Classification and seizure of dangerous artifacts
- Suppression of unrest and rebellion

## Military Capabilities
The Crown is known to possess:
- Advanced naval warships
- Arcane siege weapons
	- the [[Sunlance Crystal]]
- Experimental devices designed to control or suppress large-scale threats
	- the [[Peace Ordinance]]

The full extent of its arsenal is unknown.

## Relationship with the People
Public loyalty to the Crown remains strong in many regions, particularly [[Valoria]]. However:
- Discontent simmers among laborers and miners
- Peripheral regions report harsher enforcement
- Refugees such as the [[Fogborn]] face systemic discrimination

## Recent History
- Crackdown on unrest following events in [[Razor Ridge]]
- Increased activity in Eldorian territories
- Military intervention in [[Lowhaven]]

## Reputation
To supporters, the Crown is the last line between civilization and annihilation.

To critics, it is an authoritarian regime willing to enforce peace at catastrophic cost.
