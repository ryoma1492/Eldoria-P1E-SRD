### Phase III: The Vision (The Four Steps)

#### Step 1: The Ruin of Eldoni

The transition is instantaneous. You are standing in a major thoroughfare of Eldoni, but the capital is breaking. A choking violet static fills the sky, and the roar of collapsing stone is deafening. You feel the weight of phantom armor and the suffocating terror of a battle already lost.

Yet, the most terrifying part is the atmosphere. The city feels completely empty of life—no birds fly, no stray animals scurry through the debris. Instead, a thick, greasy malice hangs in the air, a menacing and undefined evil watching from the shadows. You know instinctively that a great shadow is here, though you cannot see it.

Driven by panic and a sudden, burning need to find an answer, Leon’s phantom self turns and sprints away from the slaughter, hurtling down the steps of a grand, crumbling estate. He descends into a subterranean library. As he runs, his eyes catch a massive, permanent mural on the wall: **Balfour I and Malazar, side-by-side, signing a fateful contract.**

Leon realizes he is hunting for something to stop this nightmare. But _where is it?_ The moment the question forms in his mind, the world warps.

#### Step 2: The Hearth

The library stretches, blurs, and snaps like a rubber band.

Suddenly, the smell of smoke and ash is gone. The air is quiet, rich with the rich, inviting aroma of baking bread and slow-simmering broth. You are standing inside a cozy, deeply lived-in home. The details here are hyper-sharp and solid.

Your gaze is pulled immediately to the stone mantlepiece over the crackling fireplace. Resting there is an object—perhaps a wand, unassuming yet radiating a dormant resonance. A wave of profound realization, a total _Eureka_ moment, washes over the party. This object is part of the solution. But it isn't the only one. _Where is the rest?_

Another thought shifts the dream.

#### Step 3: The Researcher's Records Room

The warm cottage walls dissolve outward into shadow.

Your feet are suddenly resting on familiar floorboards. The air is thick with the scent of decaying parchment and old ink. You are in a private records room—one you recognize instantly as belonging to the researcher you know.

Shelves and tables are crammed with massive stacks of scrolls and heavy, leather-bound books. Roughly half of the room is organized into meticulous, neat piles. The other half, however, is a chaotic blur of activity. Open tomes are scattered face-down, scrolls have been hastily unrolled and left askew, and ink jars sit uncapped alongside frantic, half-finished notes. The space bears the unmistakable mark of its owner having rifled through it in a state of sudden, desperate urgency.

The oppressive, unseen malice from the burning city presses against the boundaries of this room, a reminder that time is running out. You need the final piece. You think of where it must be hidden.

The shadows twist, pulling you forward one last time.

#### Step 4: The Tech Facility

The frantic records room blurs into clean, oppressive lines of cold steel and glowing ley-lines.

You are deep in the bowels of an **arcane tech facility**, located in the distant southwest corner of Eldoni. The silence here is clinical and absolute. You are standing at the end of a long, narrow room.

At the far end, resting upon a thin, austere bed, is a heavily padlocked chest. It hums with a volatile, locked-away energy.

Looking at that chest, a deep, instinctual certainty cements itself into your minds: **This is one piece of a larger trinity.** If you face the final battle in the capital without breaking into this facility and retrieving what is inside that chest first, Eldoni will burn, and the entity in the shadows will win.

---

### Phase IV: The Wake

The vision snaps like a broken mirror.

With a violent, collective gasp, you are yanked backward. The sterile tech facility vanishes, replaced by the familiar crackle of your own campfires. You are back in the circle, still holding hands, your chests heaving for breath.

In the dead center, the meticulously placed rock has gone completely dark and cold. Beneath it, the mixture of dill, salt, and the sheep's heart has been utterly consumed, reduced to nothing but a fine, pale white ash.

The future memory is yours now. And you know exactly where you must go before the end.
