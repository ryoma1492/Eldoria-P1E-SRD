# [[Savage Spire]]

## Overview
The [[Savage Spire]] is a massive inland mesa rising from the surrounding wilderness. It functions as a **managed vertical ecosystem** where the environment and its inhabitants are integrated into a single biological structure. Currently, the Spire is under systemic strain due to a natural generation-gap in **Primal Reinvigoration**.

---

## Structural Description

The Spire consists of three primary vertical zones, maintained by the specialized labor of the Kin:

### 1. Base Port Layer
- **Infrastructure:** Cliffside docks, ladder networks, and vertical cargo lifts carved into the mesa walls.
- **Ecological Role:** The interface for trade and Kin migration.
- **State:** Labor cycles are slowing as the "Thinning" reduces the physical stamina of the porters.

### 2. Mid-Span Ecosystem Layer
- **Environment:** Dense jungle growth across terraced rock formations.
- **Population:** Animal-kin labor networks (avian, feline, and mixed Kin Mutanophemes).
- **Condition:** Visible biological fatigue; the adaptive traits (climbing claws, sensory ridges) are becoming vestigial and brittle.

### 3. Summit Plateau
- **Environment:** A prehistoric ecological zone kept in artificial balance.
- **Infrastructure:** Site of **Mycelial control structures** and the **Temple Water** systems.
- **Function:** The "Brain" of the Spire, highly sensitive to external Primal resonance.

---

## The Mycelial Network
A fungal-based communication and control lattice embedded directly into the Spire’s stone and the Kin’s biology.

*   **Environmental Regulation:** Used by the Elder to maintain the terraced jungles and oxygen flow.
*   **Resonance Detection:** Capable of "pinging" Primal energy signatures from miles away.
*   **Emergency Containment:** Can be weaponized to bind or stabilize volatile forces (e.g., the [[Pocket Door]]).

### Key Entity: [[The Mycelial Elder]]
The primary interface for the Spire’s ecology. The Elder is not a ruler but a **Regulator**. It maintains the containment lattice and manages the **Temple Water Rituals** necessary to halt the "Fade" of the Kin population.

---

## Status of the Spire Kin
The Leopard-kin porters and Hawk-eyed guards are currently **Thin-Blooded**. After several generations without a **Primal Integration**, their "Mutanopheme" edge is receding.

*   **Physical Strain:** Signs of energy depletion and structural biological thinning.
*   **Awe & Hunger:** The Spire Kin sense the "Primal" (draconic) presence in the [[Heroes]] with religious reverence. To them, the party carries the "Source Code" required to trigger a **New Bloom**.

---

## Key Infrastructure & Artifacts

### The Temple Waters
Located at the summit, these hyper-conductive waters are the means of **Primal Integration**. They allow a concentrated essence (like a draconic infestation) to be "thinned" and shared among the cluster.

---

## Narrative Role: The Symbiosis of the Burden

The [[Savage Spire]] represents a critical turning point for the [[Heroes]]. It is a **live containment structure** that has become an ecosystem. 

**The Moral/Mechanical Trade:**
The Spire is no longer stable in isolation; it is fading. The [[Heroes]] possess an overwhelming Primal force that is killing one of their own. By performing the **Water Ritual** at the Spire:
1.  The **Infested Hero**  [[Captain Killian]] is relieved as the burden is thinned and shared.
2.  The **Spire Kin** are reinvigorated, halting the "Fade" and regaining their specialized forms.
3.  The **Spire** itself is stabilized as the new Primal energy reinforces the Mycelial Network.

> **The Question:** Will the party allow their "curse" to become the communal "blessing" that saves the Spire from becoming mundane?
