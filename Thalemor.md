## Description
The abandoned capital of [[Eldoria]], now consumed by fog and transformed into a massive cult stronghold.

## Plots
- Central hub of:
  - [[Crystal Cultists]]
  - [[Malazar]]’s operation
- Construction of:
  - Four pylons (regional system)
- Site of:
  - Hostage holding
  - Major ritual activation

## Citizens
- Thousands of cultists
- Enslaved / abducted civilians:
  - Including [[Annalise]]
  - Princess of [[King Balfour IV]]
- [[Malazar]] — black dragon leader

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- No traditional trade
- Functions as:
  - Ritual construction site
  - Military-religious hub
- Unique traits:
  - Massive ziggurat structure
  - Fog saturation (repelled by pyre fire)
  - Coordinated mass behavior (“The One”)
