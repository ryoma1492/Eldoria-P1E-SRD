## Core Theme
Order without mercy.

Hierarchy as perfection.
Control as virtue.
Structure above instinct.

## Faction Head
[[Arch-Marshal Seredain]]

Former holy knight turned architect of civilization.

Belief:
Freedom breeds chaos.
Chaos breeds suffering.
Suffering must be engineered away.

---

## Philosophy
- Wild magic is dangerous
- Druids are irresponsible
- Forests must be mapped and regulated
- Predators must be culled
- Spirits must be cataloged or bound

They do not consider themselves villains.

They consider themselves necessary.

---

## Signature Ability Concept

### Edict of Stillness
Within a controlled radius:

- Suppresses summoned creatures
- Reduces Wild Shape duration
- Dampens natural terrain bonuses
- Forces morale checks for animal companions
- Temporarily negates difficult terrain caused by plant growth

Visual:
Air grows heavy.
Sound dampens.
Wind halts.
