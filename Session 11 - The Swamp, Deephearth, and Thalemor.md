## Pre-Text:
With the party divided, [[Lowhaven]] stands vulnerable.

What follows is not an attack.

It is a **summons**.

## Current state
- A large portion of [[Lowhaven]]’s population is missing
- No visible enemy force
- Movement toward the swamp (toward [[Thalemor]])
- Remaining leadership uncertain

## Leg One – The Night Pull

Simultaneous events:

- [[Evan Taylor]] steals pendant from [[Braun]]
- [[Rhett]] patrols, unaware

Then:

A mass awakening.

- Townsfolk rise
- Move in unison
- Run into the swamp

Notably taken:
- [[Annalise Taylor]]

No resistance. No confusion.

Only obedience.

---

## Leg Two – Return and Revelation

Stranded party returns:

- [[Caleb]] transports allies across water (walking)
- Reunites in [[Lowhaven]]

[[Mallard]] presents:

- [[Sunlance Crystal]] gathered from the bottom of the nearby ocean floor
- Two wands -  [[magic-missile]]  and [[lightning-bolt|Lightning Bolt]]
- another of [[Fleetfin's brews]] 

The [[Heroes]] - [[Caleb]] and [[Atticus]] - are left with the wands and (as expected) start using them

Discovery:
- The crystal **stores spell energy**

---

## Leg Three – Into the Swamp

Guided by [[Farmer Jenkins]]:

Warnings:
- Worms beneath mud
- A sea serpent in deeper water

The swamp:
- Hostile terrain
- Movement dangerous

---

## Leg Four – Bud

Encounter:

- A gnome, half-buried in mud
- Introduces himself as [[Bud]]

### Key Traits

- Friendly
- Cooperative
- Deeply mistaken

Beliefs:
- The cult will “renew Emberfall”

Revelation:
- Heard a voice calling him to [[Thalemor]]

The party keeps him.

---

## Leg Five – Sunken Eldoria

The swamp gives way to:

A vast, clear lake.

Beneath it:
- Roads
- Buildings
- Entire preserved settlements

The past is still there.

Just unreachable.

---

## Leg Six – The Worm

While scouting:

- [[Bud]] is dragged under by a massive worm
- [[Caleb]] kills it instantly

Direction confirmed:
- Toward [[Thalemor]]

---

## Leg Seven – The Steeple

A tilted church tower rises from the lake.

Inside:
- A sea hag cooking fish

The party:
- Distracts it
- Collapses statue onto it
- Retrieves magical axe

Leaves quickly

---

## Leg Eight – Deephearth

A settlement built in layers:

- Each time it sinks, a new level is built above
- Connected by rope bridges

At its base:

- A **perpetual pyre**

### Properties

- Holds back fog
- Preserves the town

Warning:
- A sea serpent is drawn to it

---

## Leg Nine – The Serpent

The party lures it out.

- [[Atticus]] casts levitate
- The creature cannot dive
- Combat becomes controlled

It is slain.

### Gains

- Pyre fire (on [[Caleb]]’s shield)
- Treasure:
  - Golden lute

---

## Leg Ten – Bud’s Vision

During rest:

[[Bud]] speaks in trance:

- Four pylons:
  - [[Eldoria]]
  - [[Valoria]]
  - [[Atheria]]
  - [[Eldoni]]

A system. Not a ritual.

---

## Leg Eleven – Thalemor

The fog parts.

Revealed:

A massive ziggurat.

- Thousands of cultists
- Four pylons at corners

### Infiltration

- [[Atticus]] blends in
- [[Bud]] scouts

Findings:
- Hostages at top:
  - [[Annalise Taylor]]
  - Princess of [[King Balfour IV]]
  - Representatives of major cities

---

## Leg Twelve – Malazar

A black dragon arrives.

[[Malazar]] speaks:

- Has waited 100 years
- Fog and crystals were preparation

The cult responds in unison:

> “The One”

---

## Leg Thirteen – Escape

The party acts:

- Frees hostages
- [[Rhett]] disrupts pursuit
- [[Caleb]] clears path with fireball

Escape via:
- Dead serpent (used as raft)

---

## Leg Fourteen – The Beginning

As they flee:

- Four cyclones form
- Spread outward
- Purple lightning fills the sky

This is not an ending.

It is activation.

---

## Resolution

The party has:
- Saved key figures
- Disrupted the cult’s surface plan

But:

The system is already in motion.
