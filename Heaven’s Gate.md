## Description
A massive coastal cavern where the [[Fool’s Curtain]] meets land. One of the only known terrestrial access points to the Curtain.

## Plots
- Likely location of:
  - Crown shipment exchange
  - Annual Curtain opening
- Strong candidate for:
  - [[Granny Titchwillow]]’s map destination
- Site of party split across the Curtain

## Citizens / Entities
- No permanent population
- Temporary presence:
  - [[Royal Crown of Eldoria]] forces
  - [[Heroes]]

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- Strategic location:
  - Only land-based Curtain interaction point (by plot)
- Environmental hazard:
  - Violent current during opening
- Key trait:
  - Timing-based access (15 minutes annually)
