#### Ambrosial Lotus

**Source** PPC:WO

**Price** 25,000 gp; **Slot** none; **CL** 12th; **Weight** 50 lbs.; **Aura** strong [enchantment](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Enchantment)

DESCRIPTION

A creature who consumes an ambrosial lotus must succeed at a DC 17 [Will](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Will) save or be overtaken by such a state of total bliss so as to be almost oblivious to the world around him.

The creature’s [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) and [Charisma](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Charisma-Cha-) scores drop to 1, and the creature can no longer cast spells, understand language, or communicate. These effects are permanent unless removed with a [heal](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/heal), [limited wish](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/l/limited-wish), [miracle](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/miracle), or [wish](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/w/wish) spell.

An ambrosial lotus stalk grows one flower per day during its 13-week growing period in spring.

CULTIVATION REQUIREMENTS

**Cost** 12,500 gp; **Feats** [Cultivate Magic Plant](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/item-creation-feats/cultivate-magic-plants-item-creation/); **Spells** [feeblemind](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/feeblemind); **Special** cultivator must have 10 ranks in [Knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) (nature)





#### Palm of Decadent Feasts

**Source** [PRG:UW](http://amzn.to/2z71S07)

**Price** 9,600 gp; **Slot** none; **CL** 11th; **Weight** 500 lbs.; **Aura** strong [conjuration](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Conjuration)

DESCRIPTION

This tall tree has no branches but sprouts expansive leaves at the top. Its large, spiny fruit can be split to provide a [meal](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/equipment/goods-and-services/hunting-camping-survival-gear#TOC-Meal) for four that emulates [heroes’ feast](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/heroes-feast). If a single creature consumes the entire fruit, it gains the benefits of [heroes’ feast](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/heroes-feast) for a full 24 hours and heals twice as many [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points) of damage.

A palm of decadent feasts provides only a single fruit per year, which ripens on the summer solstice.

CULTIVATION REQUIREMENTS

**Cost** 4,800 gp; **Feats** [Cultivate Magic Plants](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/item-creation-feats/cultivate-magic-plants-item-creation); **Spells** [heroes’ feast](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/heroes-feast); **Skills** cultivator must have 6 ranks in [Knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) (nature)
