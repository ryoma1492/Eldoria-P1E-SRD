## Description
A corrupted forest south of [[Oargull]], reorganized into web-structured corridors and chambers. Sound is dampened. Movement is controlled.

## Plots
- Corruption introduced by [[MyDrider]]
- Philosophical “testing” of victims (classroom chamber)
- Connection to:
  - Lost academic culture of Port Malone
  - Twisted pursuit of logic and reason
- Cleansed (partially) by the [[Heroes]]

## Citizens / Entities
- [[Leshy]] — Original forest keeper
- [[MyDrider]] — Corrupting force (defeated)
- Ogre spider (escaped)
- Remains of “students”
- [[Althric Vale]] — Former professor turned Dreamer

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- No trade
- Unique traits:
  - Webbed environmental design
  - Logic-based encounters
  - Living “classroom” of corpses
- Symbolic function:
  - Knowledge corrupted into control
