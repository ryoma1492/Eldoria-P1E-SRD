## Description
A flooded, unstable region connecting [[Lowhaven]] to [[Sunken Eldoria]] and [[Thalemor]].

## Plots
- Path of mass migration event (mind-controlled townsfolk)
- Contains:
  - Worm threats
  - Access to deeper cult activity
- Transitional zone between:
  - Civilization and corruption

## Citizens / Creatures
- Mud worms
- Sea serpent
- Scattered cultists (traveling)

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- No trade
- Unique traits:
  - Unstable terrain (sinking hazards)
  - Hidden threats below surface
- Narrative role:
  - Threshold into deeper danger
