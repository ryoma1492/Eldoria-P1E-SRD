## Description
A layered settlement built upward as it sinks into the swamp. Stabilized by a magical flame.

## Plots
- Houses:
  - Eternal pyre (anti-fog mechanism)
- Threatened by:
  - Sea serpent
- Resource used by party:
  - Pyre fire (now weaponized by [[Caleb]])

## Citizens
- Desperate but welcoming townsfolk
- Librarian (keeper of history)
- Communal survival culture

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- Cafeteria (communal food system)
- Rope-bridge infrastructure
- Unique traits:
  - Vertical rebuilding system
  - **Ever-burning pyre beneath town**
- Strategic importance:
  - One of few places resisting the fog
