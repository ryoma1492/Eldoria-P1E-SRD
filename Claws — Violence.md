## Draconic Principle

Claws represent domination through raw predation.

This aspect embodies:
- savagery
- bloodlust
- physical supremacy
- hunting instinct

Bearers of Claws often develop aggression, territoriality, and instinctive reactions to perceived weakness.

---

# Stage I — Blood Talons

## Manifestation

The fingernails harden into blackened talons.

## Abilities

- Offhand claw attack (1d4)
- Climb speed 20 ft plus +4 bonus to climb checks
- +2 Grapple

---

# Stage II — Rending Beast

## Assimilation

The claws lengthen and become unmistakably draconic.

## Abilities

- Claws become 1d6 and can attack with both
- Gain Rend https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/universal-monster-rules#TOC-Rend-Ex-
- +4 Grapple
- Gain Scent while enraged or bloodied https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/barbarian/rage-powers/paizo-rage-powers/scent-ex/

### Predator Leap
Charge without requiring a straight line.

---

# Stage III — Tyrant Predator

## Realization

The bearer becomes a living apex predator.

## Abilities

- Claws become 1d8
- Gain Grab special attack
- Gain Pounce https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/universal-monster-rules#TOC-Pounce-Ex-
- Claws deal +1d4 acid damage

### Devastation Toss
Thrown grappled enemies:
- gains Bleed effect  https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/universal-monster-rules/#:~:text=Forum,Bleed%20(Ex)
- damage nearby creatures on collision (1d4 +1 /size category)

---

## Physical Changes

- Enlarged canine teeth
- Thickened forearms
- Altered gait
- Deepened voice
