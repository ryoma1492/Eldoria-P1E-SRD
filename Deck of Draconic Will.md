# Deck of Draconic Will

The [[Deck of Draconic Will]] represents the lingering influence of [[Malazar]] attempting to reclaim control over the fractured draconic essence carried by the [[Heroes]].

Whenever a draconic aspect is used beyond safe limits, overcharged, or invoked during moments of emotional weakness, the GM may call for a draw from this deck.

Unless otherwise stated:
- Effects occur immediately
- Save DCs scale as: `10 + 1/2 character level + highest draconic attunement stage`
- Effects caused by the deck are considered supernatural

---

# Minor Afflictions

These effects trigger immediately and are generally survivable, though disruptive.

---

## 1 — Acidic Backlash

Draconic energy destabilizes internally.

- Take `1d4 acid damage`
- Armor and clothing briefly smoke and hiss

If already bloodied:
- become Sickened for 1 round

---

## 2 — Smoldering Veins

Elemental energy escapes through the skin.

- Take `1d4 fire damage`
- Cannot benefit from invisibility until end of next turn

Small embers leak from the mouth while speaking.

---

## 3 — Predatory Twitch

Your body reacts before your mind.

- Immediately move 5 feet toward the nearest hostile creature
- This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity

You suffer `-1 AC` until your next turn.

---

## 4 — Echoing Hunger

The dragon’s instincts briefly surface.

- Become distracted by the scent of blood, fear, or weakness
- Suffer `-2 concentration` and `-2 perception` for 1 round

If an enemy is below half HP:
- you must succeed a Will save to avoid targeting them next.

---

## 5 — Eyes of the Wyrm

Your pupils become slit-like and luminous.

- Suffer `-2 Diplomacy`
- Gain `+2 Intimidate`

Effect lasts for 1 hour.

NPCs may react with fear or suspicion.

---

# Moderate Afflictions

These effects create meaningful tactical or roleplay complications.

---

## 6 — Draconic Impulse

The dragon’s instincts override caution.

For 1 round:
- cannot take Withdraw actions
- cannot willingly move away from enemies
- suffer `-2 AC`

Must move toward or attack a hostile creature if possible.

---

## 7 — Fractured Will

Whispers from [[Malazar]] flood the mind.

- Become Shaken for `1d4 rounds`
- Take `-2` on all Will saves during this duration

During moments of silence, faint draconic whispers can be heard.

---

## 8 — Sovereign Pressure

The dragon demands dominance.

For the next round:
- you cannot willingly accept aid, healing, or commands from allies
- all cooperative actions fail unless you pass a Will save

Additionally:
- gain `+2 Intimidate`
- suffer `-2 Sense Motive`

---

# Major Afflictions

These effects represent direct interference from [[Malazar]].

They should feel dangerous and memorable.

---

## 9 — Possessive Surge

The dragon briefly seizes partial control.

Must succeed a Will save or:
- the GM controls your character for 1 round

The possessed character will:
- pursue violence
- dominate weaker creatures
- protect draconic artifacts
- further the interests of [[Malazar]]

Afterward:
- gain one level of Fatigue

---

## 10 — Voice of Malazar

The ancient will fully manifests.

Must succeed a difficult Will save or become possessed for `1d4 rounds`.

While possessed:
- the GM gains full control
- the character speaks in the voice of [[Malazar]]
- allies are recognized but viewed as tools or obstacles

During this time, the possessed character:
- may expend resources recklessly
- may reveal hidden truths
- may target enemies or allies according to [[Malazar]]’s goals

After possession ends:
- character becomes Exhausted
- suffers psychic backlash (`2d6 nonlethal damage`)
- retains fragmented memories of the possession

---

# Optional Rule — Escalating Corruption

Each unresolved possession effect grants:
- +1 cumulative DC against future draws from the deck

This penalty resets only after:
- meaningful purification
- major story milestones
- divine intervention
- or resisting a possession through exceptional roleplay
