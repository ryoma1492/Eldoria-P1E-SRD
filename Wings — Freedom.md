## Draconic Principle

Wings embody the dragon's refusal to be restrained.

This aspect represents:
- mobility
- independence
- elevation
- escape
- superiority through movement

Bearers of Wings often become increasingly restless, impulsive, and resistant to confinement or authority.

---

# Stage I — Ember Wings

## Manifestation

Small scaled protrusions emerge near the shoulders or spine.

The bearer develops heightened balance and aerial instinct.

## Abilities

- +4 Acrobatics
- glide 10ft horizontally for every 5 ft vertically
- 2/day Feather Fall as a bonus action

---

# Stage II — Skirmisher Wings

## Assimilation

The wings become functional for short periods.

The bearer increasingly prefers movement over stability.

## Abilities

- Fly speed 40 ft for rounds/day equal to level
- +2 Dodge AC while moving
- Gain Flyby Attack https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/monster-feats/flyby-attack/

### Wing Buffet
As a standard action:
- Str DC 20:  push adjacent creatures 5ft per character size + 5 ft
- Bull Rush without provoking attacks of opportunity

---

# Stage III — Seraph of Ruin

## Realization

The wings fully mature into true draconic appendages.

The bearer can no longer comfortably conceal them.

## Abilities

- level/day fly speed 60 ft (Good)
- Evasion while flying https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/alternate-classes/ninja/ninja-tricks/paizo-ninja-master-tricks/evasion/
- Two wing natural attacks

### Tempest Spiral
Move through enemy spaces while flying:
- make one wing attack against each creature passed

---

## Physical Changes

- Hollowed bones
- Elongated posture
- Wings difficult to conceal
- Eyes reflect light like a predator bird
