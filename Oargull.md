## Description
A pirate town built atop the remains of **Port Malone College of the Arts**, blending maritime trade with the decaying bones of a once-intellectual society.

## Plots
- Increasing nightly attacks tied to [[Peace Ordinance]] activation
- Creatures targeting:
  - Pre-collapse artifacts
  - Meat supplies
- Possible connection to:
  - [[Crystal Cultists]]
  - Knowledge-seeking forces (linked to [[MyDrider]] and [[Althric Vale]])

## Citizens
- [[Mallard Fleetfin]] — Rogue leader, brewer
- “Jaggery” — Negotiator/businessman
- Alaria Fleetfin — Trusted second
- General pirate population
- Former academic echoes (ruins, remnants)

## Trade & Unique Qualities
- Massive brewery (rum + specialty stout)
- Dry dock (ship repair hub)
- Pirate market (access to magic items)
- Casino ship (economic/social center)
- Known for:
  - **Pirate Accord token system**
  - Salvage operations
- Cultural tone:
  - Practical, opportunistic, strained by recent attacks
