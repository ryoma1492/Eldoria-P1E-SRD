# Breath — Power

## Draconic Principle

Breath embodies destruction made manifest.

This aspect represents:
- overwhelming force
- magical wrath
- elemental supremacy
- catastrophic presence

Bearers of Breath often become emotionally volatile and increasingly comfortable with collateral destruction.

---

# Stage I — Smoldering Core

## Manifestation

Heat, frost, smoke, or sparks leak from the bearer during emotional stress.

## Abilities

- Breath Weapon 3/day
- 15-ft cone or 20-ft line
- 3d6 elemental damage
- Resist 5 matching energy

---

# Stage II — Furnace Heart

## Assimilation

The elemental power stabilizes and expands.

## Abilities

- Breath Weapon deals 6d6
- Recharge 1d4 rounds
- Sculpt around allies

### Elemental Infusion
Imbue wielded weapon with elemental burst damage.

---

# Stage III — Cataclysm Incarnate

## Realization

The bearer channels devastation like a true dragon.

## Abilities

- Breath Weapon deals 10d6
- Immunity to matching energy
- Secondary status effect on failed save

### Overcharge Breath
Maximize Breath Weapon damage.

Using this ability requires two draws from the [[Deck of Draconic Will]].

---

## Physical Changes

- Smoke or frost escapes while speaking
- Veins glow faintly
- Teeth sharpen
- Voice gains unnatural resonance
