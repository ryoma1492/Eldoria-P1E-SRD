## Draconic Principle

Eyes embody the dragon's supernatural awareness.

This aspect represents:
- perception
- hidden knowledge
- dominance of the mind
- seeing weakness

Bearers of Eyes often become paranoid, observant, and increasingly difficult to deceive.

---

# Stage I — Hunter's Eyes

## Manifestation

The pupils narrow into reptilian slits.

## Abilities

- Darkvision 60 ft
- +5 Perception
- Detect Magic at will

---

# Stage II — Veilpiercer

## Assimilation

The bearer begins instinctively noticing hidden movement and deception.

## Abilities

- See Invisibility
- Blindsense 20 ft
- +2 Initiative

### Dread Glance
Target becomes Shaken on failed Will save.

---

# Stage III — Sovereign Gaze

## Realization

The eyes become unmistakably draconic.

## Abilities

- Blindsight 15 ft
- True Seeing for minutes/day equal to level
- Immunity to illusion-based confusion

### Sovereign Command
Choose one:
- Dominate Person 1/day
OR
- Suggestion 3/day

---

## Physical Changes

- Reflective eyeshine
- Reduced blinking
- Eye color shifts toward draconic hues
